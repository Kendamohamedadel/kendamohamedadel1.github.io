About

Kenda Mohamed Adel is one of Egypt's rising Taekwondo champions. She won the Egyptian National Championship twice and continues to pursue international success while proudly representing Egypt. Her ambition is to represent the Egyptian National Team and compete internationally, proudly carrying the Egyptian flag.. Current Weight: 23 kg
